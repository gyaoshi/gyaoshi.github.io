OmniSearch Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

English 中文

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how OmniSearch ("the App") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Information the App Collects

OmniSearch does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All user data, including search engine configurations and preferences, is stored locally on your device using Android Room database.

Information Collected by Third Parties

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following information for ad personalization and delivery:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For details, please refer to Google Privacy Policy.

Local Data Storage

All data generated within the App is stored locally on your device:

  • Search engine configurations — URLs, names, and icons of your custom search engines
  • User preferences — App settings and theme choices

This data is stored in a local Room database and never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to load search results and display advertisements
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required to check network connectivity status

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and are not used to collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — Used to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad targeting. See Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

简介

本隐租政策说明了 OmniSearch（“本应用”）如何处理用户数据。我们重视您的隐租保护，并对数据处理方式保持透明。

信息收集

应用收集的信息

OmniSearch 不会收集、传输或共享任何个人数据。所有用户数据（包括搜索引擎配置和偏好设置）均通过 Android Room 数据库存储在您的设备本地。

第三方收集的信息

本应用通过 Google AdMob 展示广告。AdMob 可能会收集以下信息用于广告个性化和投放：

  • 设备标识符（广告 ID）
  • 设备信息（型号、操作系统版本、屏幕尺寸）
  • 近似位置（基于 IP 地址）
  • 广告交互数据（展示、点击）

详细信息请参阅 Google 隐租政策。

本地数据存储

应用内产生的所有数据均存储在您的设备本地：

  • 搜索引擎配置 — 自定义搜索引擎的 URL、名称和图标
  • 用户偏好 — 应用设置和主题选择

这些数据存储在本地 Room 数据库中，不会离开您的设备。卸载应用将永久删除所有本地存储的数据。

权限说明

本应用请求以下 Android 权限：

  • INTERNET — 用于加载搜索结果和展示广告
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — 用于检查网络连接状态

这些权限仅用于上述用途，不会用于收集个人信息。

第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务：

  • Google AdMob — 用于展示横带广告。AdMob 可能会收集设备信息用于广告定向。详见 Google 隐租政策 和 AdMob 政策。

儿童隐租

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。如果您是家长或监护人，认为您的孩子提供了个人信息，请联系我们以便我们采取适当措施。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐租政策。任何变更将发布在本页面并更新生效日期。建议您定期查看本政策。
